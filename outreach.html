Outreach

I am actively engaged in science outreach and communication, with a focus on making planetary science accessible to non-specialist audiences. Alongside my core interest in space science, I enjoy engaging with adjacent topics such as gemmology and environmental science, as well as broader themes in general science.

Selected Outreach Activities

Co-founder, Astronomy on Tap Zurich
I am a co-founder of Astronomy on Tap Zurich, a public outreach event series that brings scientists and the general public together in informal settings.

Invited outreach talk, St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai
I delivered an invited talk aimed at promoting exoplanet science to undergraduate students at the Department of Geology, St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai.

Fantasy Basel – Swiss Comic Con
I participated in public outreach activities at Fantasy Basel, supporting the Space Pavilion at the NCCR PlanetS stand.
