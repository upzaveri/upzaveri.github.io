Welcome

I’m a PhD candidate at Experimental Planetology Group in ETH Zürich.

My research focuses on how planets form, what they are made of, and how their interiors evolve. I am particularly interested in rocky exoplanets and planetary systems that form under reducing chemical conditions.

Alongside research, I am passionate about science outreach and public engagement. I really enjoy translating complex scientific ideas into accessible stories and creating spaces where science feels curious and fun.
