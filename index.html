Welcome

Welcome to my website. I’m Urja Zaveri, a PhD candidate at ETH Zurich.

My research focuses on how planets form, what they are made of, and how their interiors evolve. I am particularly interested in rocky exoplanets and planetary systems that form under reducing chemical conditions. My work ranges from thermodynamic and interior modelling to conducting high-pressure temperature experiments.

Alongside research, I am passionate about science outreach and public engagement. I really enjoy translating complex scientific ideas into accessible stories and creating spaces where science feels curious and fun.
